How It Works

01

Apply & Qualify

Complete a short eligibility check. We work with young people from the most deprived areas in the UK.

02

Complete the Programme

Work through our presentation packs on emotional intelligence, interviews, networking, and more. You have two weeks.

03

Interview & Get Placed

Present what you've learned. If you're ready, we connect you directly with our corporate partners.
